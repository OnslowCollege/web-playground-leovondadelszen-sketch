Carlos Alcaraz

Carlos Alcaraz is a Spanish professional tennis player who has rapidly risen to prominence in the sport. Born on May 5, 2003, in Murcia, Spain, Alcaraz turned professional in 2018 and has since made significant strides on the ATP Tour. Known for his aggressive playing style, powerful groundstrokes, and exceptional athleticism, he has drawn comparisons to tennis legends like Rafael Nadal. Alcaraz achieved his first ATP title in 2021 and has continued to build on that success with multiple tournament victories. As of 2024, he is considered one of the most promising young talents in tennis, with a bright future ahead of him as he competes at the highest levels of the sport.
